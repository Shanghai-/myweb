Nice to meet you!

I am a Brown University graduate with an ScB. in Computer Science. I enjoy developing beautiful, responsive applications and hardware for wide consumer use. I have worked with a wide array of languages and platforms, and in both large and small teams. I love super-collaborative working environments. My goal is to become a knowledgable and flexible developer who can address any need which should arise.

In my spare time, I like to play, critically analyze, and create video games. I also make music and sound art, and have released an album on Bandcamp. I play & run tabletop roleplaying games. I love theater, architecture, and comedy. I also like to build things - papercraft, IOT projects, costume making, and more. And I've been working hard and getting better at 3D modeling, animating, and sculpting recently.
