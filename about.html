About

I am a current sophomore at Brown University working towards an ScB. in Computer Science. I love making tools to help people get things done smarter, whether those things are unique, creative endeavors or just picking which dining hall to get lunch at. I have worked with Java, Javascript, Scala, Swift, Objective-C, C#, PHP, MySQL, OCaml, and HTML/CSS. I'm experienced in object-oriented, imperative, and functional programming. I've developed for both mobile and desktop, and I'm teaching myself web development. My goal is to become a knowledgable and flexible developer who can address any need which should arise.

In my spare time, I like to play, analyze, and make video games. I'm a member of the Brown/RISD Game Dev team and the Rube Goldberg Machine Team. I love theater, architecture, and good design.
